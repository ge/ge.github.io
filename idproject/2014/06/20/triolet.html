Triolet

Three sisters:

either side of thirty, wear smart hats

Warding off heat:

and whistling men, they walk in Athens'

backstreets:

where old women shake out doormats

when these three sisters:

pass by in their smart hats

Men snap their fingers:

near them are prowling alley cats

and from open doorways:

woman flap stained aprons

Three sisters:

either side of thirty, wearing smart hats

ward off heat:

and whistling men while they walk in Athens

-D
