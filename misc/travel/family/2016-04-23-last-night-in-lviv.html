Last Night in Lviv (2016-04-23)

Apr 23, 2016

The Camera Café

Our hotel was just a few doors away from this café. So we just had to stop there for coffee and tea on our last afternoon in Lviv.

And of course we had to have our last dinner in Amadeus. It was a spectacular meal for about $35 including wine and beer. And escargot. And salad. And dessert. And I don’t remember what.
